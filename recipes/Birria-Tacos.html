Birria Tacos

Ingredients

  • Short ribs(Bone in)
  • Chuck roast
  • Corn tortillas
  • White onion
  • Chicken bouillon
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Bay leaves
  • Quesadilla cheese
  • Guajillo peppers
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Chili Powder

Steps

  1. To start preparing the veggies halve the onion, slice the carrots, cut the tip off the garlic bulb, cut off stem of peppers and remove seeds.
  2. Add ALL INGREDIENTS to a large pot except the chili powder. Cover with water and let simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Skim the top of the broth to remove any foam like impurities that might have come for the bones while simmering.
  4. Remove the peppers from the broth and blend them until smooth. Adding a small amount of broth if needed for texture.
  5. Continue to let the stew simmer for about 3 hours until the meat is tender and can be shredded easily. Stir around every 40 minutes while simmering.
  6. Skim the grease from the top of the broth and save it in a bowl.
  7. Remove the meat from the broth and finely chop it.
  8. Dip your corn tortillas into the grease you saved and then fry them over medium heat. While frying top the tortillas with your chopped beef and cheese. While frying fold the taco over and press down. Fry unitl both sides are crisp
  9. If you want to serve your tacos with consomme, spoon some of the broth into a bowl and top it with cilantro and diced onion.
  10. Now enjoy your delicious beef birria tacos!
